About Qis College of Engineering & Technology

Established in 1998 under Sri Nidamanuri Educational Society, QISCET is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK) and approved by AICTE.

Located near Ongole in a serene green campus environment, the institution focuses on academic excellence, industry collaboration, innovation-driven learning, and holistic student development.

The college offers Undergraduate and Postgraduate programs with strong laboratory infrastructure, digital classrooms, research support, and consistent placement records.

Accreditations & Approvals:

  • Approved by AICTE
  • Permanently affiliated to JNTU
  • Accredited by NBA
  • Accredited by NAAC with A+ grade
  • UGC Recognised

Admission Procedure

The eligibility criteria for admission into UG & PG programmes are as per the norms approved by Government of Andhra Pradesh from time to time. The sanctioned seats in each program in the college are classified into CATEGORY – A and CATEGORY – B at first year level and Lateral Entry at second year level.

QIS College of engineering & Technology – EAMCET Code: QISE

Reservation Policy – Seats will be filled by the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh as per the Govt. approved Policy.

Category A Seats: 70% of seats will be filled by the Government of Andhra Pradesh as per the norms approved.

Category B Seats: 30% of seats will be filled by the College as per the norms approved by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

Category Lateral Entry Seats: Lateral entry candidates shall be admitted into the Third semester directly as per the norms approved by Government of Andhra Pradesh.

NOTE: The percentages of Category – A, Category – B and Lateral Entry Seats are decided from time to time by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.
